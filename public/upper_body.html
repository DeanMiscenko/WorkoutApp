Upper body exercises

Filter by body part below, or search for a specific exercise in the navbar.

Pull-up

Back

Vertical pulling movement targeting lats, upper back and biceps. Can be done bodyweight or assisted.

Barbell row

Back

Hip-hinged horizontal row focusing on mid-back and lats, with some posterior-chain stability.

Face pull

Shoulders

Cable or band pull to the face that strengthens rear delts and external rotators.

Overhead press

Shoulders

Pressing movement overhead, targeting front and side delts and triceps.

Barbell curl

Biceps

Standing curl focusing on elbow flexion and biceps size and strength.

Hammer curl

Biceps

Neutral-grip curl emphasizing brachialis and forearm muscles.

Skullcrusher

Triceps

Lying triceps extension to target long head and overall triceps strength.

Cable pushdown

Triceps

Cable movement isolating triceps with controlled lockout.

Wrist curl

Forearms

Seated curl variation focusing on wrist flexors and grip endurance.

Reverse curl

Forearms

Overhand grip curl targeting brachioradialis and wrist extensors.

Bench press

Chest

Horizontal press for chest, shoulders and triceps, often used as a strength benchmark.

Incline dumbbell press

Chest

Incline variation emphasizing upper chest and front delts.
